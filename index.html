What is the Internet


The Internet is a global network of interconnected computers and devices that communicate with each other using standard communication protocols. It allows people around the world to share information, communicate, and access online services.

The Internet connects millions of devices, including computers, smartphones, tablets, and servers. When a user requests information, such as opening a website, the request travels through the Internet to a server, which sends the requested data back to the user's device.
